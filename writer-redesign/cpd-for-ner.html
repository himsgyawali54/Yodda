What is CPD for NER?

CPD for NER is documentation of activities that directly address the NER competency standards.

It is an important document required during the NER Assessment process. With an accurately written CPD, the chances of becoming registered in the National Engineering Register become higher.

Requirement for NER CPD:

  • At least 150 hours of CPD activities in the previous three years.
  • 50 hours of CPD activities in the last 12 months

These activities must cover every CPD Types and Conditions.

Breakdown of CPD hours:

  • 50 hours related to your area of practice
  • 10 hours that cover risk management
  • 15 hours of CPD dedicated to business and management skills
  • 75 hours of CPD activities include activities relevant to your career interests

CPD For CDR: Things To Consider

1/4

Relevance

Ensure CPD activities are relevant to your NER Area of Practice.

2/4

Cover CPD Hours

Cover 150 CPD hours in the last three years OR at least 50 hours in the last twelve months before applying.

3/4

Consistency

Demonstrate consistent dedication, efforts, and participation in CPD activities.

4/4

Variation

Highlight participation in individual and group activities. Show your comfort in working alone as well as in a group.

Our NER CPD Format

For your NER Assessment, we follow the standard format. This format includes:

  • Ref
  • Date
  • CPD Type (I to VIII)
  • CPD activity / topic / provider
  • How an activity has extended your knowledge?
  • Risk Management
  • Business & Management
  • Related Area of Practice
  • Hours
