Most Employable Degrees In Australia For 2023

Many students and graduates are looking to pursue careers that offer promising job prospects and opportunities for growth. In Australia, the job market continues to evolve, with certain industries experiencing high demand for skilled professionals. Among these industries, engineering stands out as one of the most sought-after fields of study, with various specialties that offer excellent career prospects. This article will explore six of Australia's most employable engineering degrees for 2023.

Most Employable Degrees In Australia

1. Civil Engineering

Scope

Civil Engineering is a broad field that involves designing, constructing, and maintaining infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. Civil Engineers may work on transportation, water resources, environmental, and geotechnical engineering projects.

Salary of Civil Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for a Civil Engineer in Australia is AU$ 78,492 per year.

Advantages

A degree in civil engineering provides a wide range of opportunities for career development, with roles available in both the public and private sectors. Civil engineers also positively impact society as they contribute to developing safe and sustainable infrastructure that benefits the community. Additionally, civil engineering is considered a highly respected profession, which can lead to increased recognition and credibility in the public eye.

2. Software Engineering

Scope

Software engineering is a rapidly growing field that involves designing, developing, and maintaining software applications and systems. Software engineers may work on web development, mobile app development, artificial intelligence, and cybersecurity projects.

Salary of Software Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for a Software Engineer in Australia is AU$ 82,483 per year.

Advantages

A software engineering degree offers excellent career development prospects, with strong demand for skilled professionals. The tech industry is highly innovative and dynamic, providing opportunities for personal and professional growth. Additionally, software engineering is a field that is closely associated with entrepreneurship and innovation, which can be attractive to potential employers and investors.

3. Mechanical Engineering

Scope

Mechanical engineering is a diverse field that involves designing, manufacturing, and maintaining mechanical systems such as engines, machines, and tools. Mechanical Engineers may work on robotics, automation, energy systems, and aerospace engineering projects.

Salary of Mechanical Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for a Mechanical Engineer in Australia is AU$ 76,920 per year.

Advantages

A Mechanical Engineering degree offers various career development opportunities, with roles available in various automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries. Mechanical engineers also have the opportunity to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technologies that can have a positive impact on society. Additionally, mechanical engineering is a highly transferable skill set with potential renewable energy and sustainability applications.

4. Electrical Engineering

Scope

Electrical engineering is a field that involves the design, development, and maintenance of electrical systems, including electronic devices, power generation, transmission and distribution systems, and control systems. Electrical Engineers may work on renewable energy, robotics, telecommunications, and automation projects.

Salary of Electrical Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for an Electrical Engineer in Australia is AU$ 78,928 per year.

Advantages

A degree in electrical engineering offers excellent opportunities for career development, with roles available in a range of industries such as energy, telecommunications, and manufacturing. Electrical engineering is a field that is closely associated with innovation and technological advancement, which can be attractive to potential employers and investors. Additionally, electrical engineering is a highly transferable skill set with potential renewable energy and sustainability applications.

5. Chemical Engineering

Scope

Chemical engineering is a field that involves the design, development, and optimization of processes for the production of chemicals, materials, and energy. Chemical engineers may work on pharmaceuticals, food and beverage production, oil and gas, and environmental engineering projects.

Salary of Chemical Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for a Chemical Engineer in Australia is AU$ 73,913 per year.

Advantages

A degree in chemical engineering provides excellent prospects for career development, with strong demand for skilled professionals in this field. Chemical engineers work in various industries, including energy, food and beverage production, and environmental engineering. Chemical engineering is a field that is closely associated with innovation and sustainability, which can be attractive to potential employers and investors. Additionally, chemical engineering is a highly transferable skill set with potential applications in renewable energy and advanced materials.

6. Aerospace Engineering

Scope

Aerospace Engineering is a field that involves the design, development, and maintenance of aircraft and spacecraft. Aerospace engineers may work on commercial aviation, defense systems, and space exploration projects.

Salary of Aerospace Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for an Aerospace Engineer in Australia is AU$ 82,396 annually.

Advantages

A degree in aerospace engineering provides excellent prospects for career development, with roles available in various industries such as aviation, defense, and space exploration. Aerospace engineering is a field that is closely associated with innovation and technological advancement, which can be attractive to potential employers and investors. Additionally, aerospace engineering is a highly specialized skill set with potential applications in emerging fields such as space tourism and exploration. You can contact us to know more about this topic.

7. Biomedical Engineering

Scope

Biomedical engineering is a field that involves the application of engineering principles to the development of medical devices and technologies, as well as the study of biological systems. Biomedical engineers may work on medical imaging, prosthetics, tissue engineering, and drug delivery projects.

Salary of Biomedical Engineer

According to PayScale, the average salary for a Biomedical Engineer in Australia is AU$ 69,379 per year.

Advantages

A biomedical engineering degree offers excellent career development opportunities, with roles available in various industries such as healthcare, medical device manufacturing, and pharmaceuticals. Biomedical engineering is a rapidly growing field, with increasing demand for skilled professionals in medical imaging, tissue engineering, and drug delivery. Biomedical engineering is a field that is closely associated with innovation and improving human health, which can be attractive to potential employers and investors.
