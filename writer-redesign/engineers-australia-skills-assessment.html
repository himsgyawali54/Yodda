Engineers Australia Skills Assessment Complete Guide 2023

Engineer Australia is the authorized body working for the professional advancement of the engineer sector in Australia. It provides the skills assessment to Engineering applicants with accredited qualifications and non-accredited qualifications option for skilled migration purposes. Depending on the accredited and non-accredited qualifications there are varieties of pathways for Engineers Australia skill assessment. Our company CDR Writer Australia and our team are here to guide you through the EA skill assessment process for Australian immigration purposes. If you want an Engineers Australia migration skills assessment, it is necessary to meet the eligibility criteria specified.

What is Engineers Australia Skills Assessment?

Engineers Australia Skills Assessment is a process through which Engineers Australia, the professional association representing the engineering profession in Australia, evaluates the qualifications and work experience of engineers who are seeking to migrate to Australia, or those who have completed their engineering education overseas and wish to work in Australia.

The employment assessment is conducted by Engineers Australia's Migration Skills Assessment (MSA) team, which reviews the applicant's qualifications, work experience, and other relevant factors. The MSA team may also conduct interviews or request additional information from the applicant to support their assessment.

Who needs EA Skills Assessment?

EA Skills Assessment is required to those who wish to immigrate to Australia under the skilled migration program who have credentials and expertise in engineering-related sectors such as civil, electrical, mechanical, chemical, and other engineering specialties.These evaluation abilities are crucial for determining whether a person's credentials and experience are appropriate and can match the Australian level for the occupation they wished to work in.

You will probably need to go through the Skill Assessment Engineers Australia procedure if you have training and experience in engineering or similar professions and wish to work in Australia. This will make it more likely that your abilities and expertise will be acknowledged in Australia and that you will be qualified to work in the occupation of your choice.

1. Eligibility for Skill Assessment Engineers Australia:

The individual must full fill following requirements to be eligible for Engineers Australia Migration Skills Assessment:

a. Education and Work Experience

An individual should have a complete relevant engineering degree and be recognized by a relevant professional body or authority from a recognized institution and must have a minimum of two years of relevant work experience as an engineer and experience must be verified by a professional reference.

b. English Language Proficiency

Individuals must take English Language Proficiency such as IELTS or TOEFL and must have a good score level according to countries criteria.

c. Professional Registration

Individuals must be registered as professional Engineers from the relevant professional body of their own country.

d. Character and Health Requirements

Individuals must meet the character and health requirements set by their own country, also the police report and medical examination.

There are two pathways for the Engineers Australia Migration Skill Assessment which are given as below:

a. Accredited Qualification Pathways

The following are the pathways with accredited qualification applicant for migration skill assessment:-

  • Australian Qualification Pathways
  • Washington Accord Pathways
  • Sydney Accord Pathways
  • Dublin Accord Pathways

The following are the pathways with accredited qualification applicant for migration skill assessment:-

  • Competency Demonstration Report (CDR)
  • Technical Report
  • Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL Report)

4. Prepare your Application

For preparing your applications you can go through the link Prepare your migration skills assessment application.

5. Submit your Applications

If you are submitting online you need to create an account in the EA Portal. You will receive your account ID via email once you create your account. When you receive your ID Login you need and select migration skills assessment from the menu. Select your assessment pathway and uploadall the required documents and submit your application. The online application is easy, but if you need any help you can refer to the Online Application Guide. When your application will be successfully submitted youâ€™ll receive an email confirmation.

6. EA Skill Assessment Processing Time

The application of Engineers Australia Migration Skills Assessment generally takes about 8 to 12 weeks to be assigned to an assessor. The time it takes depends upon the quality of your documents or if anything goes wrong you need to provide your document again by correcting. You can Login to your account on the EA portal to check your application status. You can also email us to know the status of your application.

7. Verify Your Skill Assessment Outcome:

To verifying your EA skill Assessment Outcome, you need to visit the Engineers Australia portal. After that, log in to your account by entering your Last name, EA ID, and Application ID, which are mandatory fields. The optional field, Department of Home Affairs Reference, is only required for Department of Home Affairs Staff Members.

8. Receive your Assessment Result

For your application decision, you will receive an email and if your application is successful you will receive a Positive migration skills assessment outcome letter which will be used for your migrational purpose. Your employment assessment letter will be valid for an indefinite period with Engineers Australia, for that you need to check the Department of Home Affairs Website to know the valid time for migration visa applications. If you need information about migration or all questions about visa - related issues you should contact the Department of Home Affairs.

9. Outcome Reviews

If you are not satisfied with your outcome result you can request a Review of your EA skill assessment outcome within three months of receiving it. It is conducted by an independent assessor and gives advice via email.It takes up to 8 weeks.

You can check the reviews and appeals fees on the assessment fees and additional services page.

How long does EA skill assessment take?

The time it takes for Engineers Australia to assess your skills depends on the type of assessment, how complex your case is, and how long it takes for them to get all the necessary documents. It usually takes about 12 weeks after they receive everything they need. It may take longer if there are any missing or incomplete documents. If you need a faster assessment, you can pay for the Fast Track process, which takes about 20 working days but costs extra.

Check out our Free Fast Track Process blog for more information about EA skill assessment process.

Why is the EA Skill Assessment Necessary?

When making a request for a skilled visa, Engineers Australia's skills evaluation is required. It demonstrates that you are a certified engineering expert with the required skills. Your visa application to the Department of Home Affairs will be accompanied by your acceptance letter from Engineers Australia. Additionally, depending on how many years of relevant job experience you have in your sector, you could be able to earn more points. You should definitely claim the maximum amount of points for your post-qualification job experience, according to the Clever Migration Club.
